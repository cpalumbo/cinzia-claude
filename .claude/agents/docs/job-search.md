## Track 2: Ex-Founder / Former Founder Roles

### Why This Track Matters
A growing number of startups are explicitly hiring "ex-founders" or "former founders" as a distinct job category. These roles value the unique skillset founders bring: ownership mentality, comfort with ambiguity, customer obsession, and ability to build from scratch.

### Why This Fits Your Profile
- VC-backed founder experience (Speedinvest, Expa) â†’ exactly what these roles seek
- Scaled from 0 â†’ proven ability to build in ambiguous environments
- Technical + commercial hybrid â†’ can bridge product and GTM
- Hands-on operator â†’ not a career corporate person
- Startup ecosystem native â†’ understand the founder journey

### Types of Ex-Founder Roles that are fitting

**1. Ex-Founder GTM / Sales / Ops**
- Focus: Sales, operations, go-to-market, owning revenue end-to-end
- Example: Soff - "Ex-Founder Turned Sales, Ops & GTM Pro"
- Comp: $80K-$200K + 0.5-1% equity
- Best for: Founders who led commercial functions

**2. Enterprise Sales Lead (Ex-founder)**
- Focus: Own strategic customer relationships, build sales engine
- Example: Kaedim (London) - "Great fit for an ex-founder or early builder who enjoys working closely with customers"
- Best for: Founders with B2B/enterprise experience

### Common Requirements in Ex-Founder Job Descriptions
```
- "Scaled from 0 to X amount in ARR" or "built and scaled a SaaS or AI-driven product"
- "Ex-founder or founding team member"
- "Technical fluency" (even for non-technical roles)
- "Comfortable owning revenue/problems end-to-end"
- "Happy working with limited process"
- "Building GTM motion in a fast-moving environment"
- "Strong autonomy and ownership mindset"
```
