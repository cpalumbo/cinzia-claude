## Quick Selection Guide

**By Budget Level**

| Budget | Best Channels |
|--------|---------------|
| $0-1K/mo | Content, Social, Community, SEO |
| $1-5K/mo | + Email, Speaking, Partnerships |
| $5-20K/mo | + Paid Ads, Influencers, Events |
| $20K+/mo | + PR, Trade Shows, Full Sales |

**By Time to Results**

| Timeframe | Best Channels |
|-----------|---------------|
| <1 month | Sales, Paid Ads, Partnerships |
| 1-3 months | Email, Influencers, Speaking |
| 3-6 months | Content, SEO, Community |
| 6+ months | PR, Viral Mechanics, Brand |

**By Company Stage**

| Stage | Priority Focus |
|-------|----------------|
| Pre-revenue | Sales, Content, Community |
| Early revenue | Paid Ads, Partnerships, Email |
| Growth | SEO, PR, Programs (Affiliate/Referral) |
| Scale | Full-funnel, Brand, Events |
