# Job Research: Executive Roles in Robotics, AI & Deep Tech

This research is designed to identify:
Explicit executive roles aligned with Head of GTM / commercial leadership
Companies entering a GTM transition phase, even if no relevant role is publicly posted yet
The goal is to enable early, proactive outreach to founders, CEOs, and boards when AND before GTM roles are formalized

## Target Profile

### Candidate Summary
- VC-backed founder and GTM lead with experience in Deep Tech, including Developer Tools, AI, scientific computing, drones, and mobile robotics
- 5 years at DJI (drones/robotics) including leading global strategic partnerships and international marketing teams
- Core skills: general management, P&L ownership, fundraising, cross-functional team management, GTM strategy, recruiting.
- GTM expertise: experienced in strategic partnerships, BD, business development, marketing, demand generation, sales, media relations, government relations, developer relations, spokesperson.
- Technical comfort: working with engineering & product teams (use of Jira, Github), AI tooling like Claude Code and Cursor for web development and automations; comfortable with modern product, sales, and marketing stacks (analytics, CRM, prospecting and sales tools, microsoft office), 
- Languages: English, Spanish, Italian (native), Mandarin Chinese (intermediate)
- International: experience working in China, US, LATAM, EU

### Target Roles
```
head_of_gtm, gtm_lead, head_of_go_to_market, head_of_growth, head_of_business_development, 
head_of_partnerships, head_of_strategic_partnerships, head_of_commercial, head_of_sales,
vp_gtm, vp_go_to_market, vp_growth, vp_business_development, vp_partnerships, vp_commercial,
chief_operating_officer, chief_commercial_officer, chief_revenue_officer,

```

### Target Industries
- Mobile robotics (drones, AMRs, warehouse automation, delivery robots)
- AI/ML (applied AI, AI infrastructure, MLOps, AI agents)
- Developer tools / DevOps / infrastructure
- Deep tech (scientific computing, hardware+software, climate tech, biotech)
- Adjacent: aerospace, autonomous vehicles, industrial automation

### Target Company Stage
- Series A to Series C (primary)
- Late seed with traction (selective)
- Growth-stage scale-ups (50-500 employees)
- Corporate spin-offs / ventures in robotics/AI

### Location & Scope
- **Primary:** Barcelona, London
- **Open to:** Remote-first with travel, or HQ in EU/UK with international scope
- **Travel:** International travel expected and welcomed
- **Dealbreaker:** Roles limited to single domestic market

## What to Capture in the CSV file (the variables below should be columns)

### Job Basics
- company_name
- company_description (what they do)
- industry_vertical (robotics, drones, EV, biotech, AI, devtools, quantum, etc.)
- job_title
- job_url
- location
- date_posted
- fit_score (high, medium, low based on candidate profile)

### Company Context
- company_stage (seed, series_a, series_b, series_c, growth)
- total_funding (e.g. "$100M")
- employee_count
- job_listings_count (no.of current job openings)
- gtm_maturity_signal (hiring_GTM_roles | only_hiring_technical_roles)
- key_investors (lead investors last rounds)
